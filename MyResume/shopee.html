Shopee Code League 2020

Details

  • Category: Coding Competition
  • Company: Shopee
  • Project date: June 2020 - Aug 2020
  • Project URL: https://careers.shopee.sg/codeleague/

From June to August 2020, I participated in Shopee's annual coding competition with a group of friends from SMU. Unlike last year's "I'm the best coder 2019" competition, this spanned 8 weeks, allowing participants to choose the competitions that they wanted to join. This allowed me to select challenges that I was familiar with, such as the classification challenge using deep neural networks, which allowed me to use knowledge gained from my internship at Bosch. Additionally, there were also more straightforward challenges which were solving problem statements within a certain time limit.

The challenges were really an eye-opener to understand the potential difficulties that might be faced while managing an e-commerce platform, such as fraud or merchandise logisitics. Additionally, the efficient solutions suggested by other teams gave me motivation to improve my critical-thinking skills, especially under a stressful situation.

In conclusion, the competition was a great experience to familiarise myself with coding under time constraints as well as persevering with challenges that might not be solved within a day or two.
